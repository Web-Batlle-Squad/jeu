Rules

The game's goal is to kill all enemies. To defeat your opponents, you will have limited moves and attacks. Obstacles will block your way to prevent you from killing them.

Characters

Knight

After he lost his childhood friend in a terrible battle to defend his kingdom, this bloodthirsty fighter promised to kill without mercy all his opponents.

Knights are stronger against bowmen.

Archer

Having grown up in the forest, he used to hunt animals with his bow to survive. After many long years of hunting, he became an exceptional shooter and never misses his target.

Bowmen are stronger against spearmans.

Spearman

His spear belonged to his father and was his last gift before his death, the spearman promise to become the mightiest warrior with his father's spear.

Spearmans are stronger against knights.

Obstacles

Rock

This big rock will become your worst enemy, we created it just to annoy you.

You can't move on a case with a rock.

Fence

Their unique goal : waste your time

You can cross the fence, but you will need 4 points of stamina.

Enemies

Kill all the enemies and win the battle !
