Rules

The aim of the game is to kill all the enemies. The difficulties are to defeat the opponents with limited moves and attacks. Also there are field obstacles that you can't cross.

Characters

Knight

After he lost his childhood friend in a terrible battle to defend his kingdom, this bloodthirsty fighter promised to kill without mercy all this opposants.

The knight is stronger against the archer.

Archer

Having grown up in the forest, he used to hunt animals with his bow to survey. After long years of hunt he became an exceptional shooter who can kill an enemy at the other side of the battlefield.

The archer is stronger against the spearman.

Spearman

His spear belonged at his father and was his last gift before his death, the spearman promise to become the mightiest warrior with his father's spear.

The spearman is stronger against the knight.

Obstacles

Rock

This big rock will become your worst enemy, we created it just to block you.

You can't move on a case with a rock.

Fence

Their unique goal : waste your time

You can cross the fence, but you will need 4 points of stamina.

Enemies

Kill all the enemies and win the battle !
