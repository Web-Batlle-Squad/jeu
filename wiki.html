Rules

At the start of the game, you have a total of 30 points of Stamina. The aim of the game is to kill all the enemies. The difficulties are to defeat the opponents with limited moves and attacks. Also there are field obstacles that you can't cross.

Characters

Knight

After he lost his childhood friend in a terrible battle to defend his kingdom, this bloodthirsty fighter promised to kill without mercy all this opposants.

Archer

Having grown up in the forest, he used to hunt animals with his bow to survey. After long years of hunt he became an exceptional shooter who can kill an enemy at the other side of the battlefield.

Spearman

His spear belonged at his father and was his last gift before his death, the spearman promise to become the mightiest warrior with his father's spear.

Enemies

Static enemies will block your road to the goal and try to kill you if you walk near from them.

Obstacles

This big tree will become your worst enemy, moreover block your road, your archers can't shoot when they are behind
